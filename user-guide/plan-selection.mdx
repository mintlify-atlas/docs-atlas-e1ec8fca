## Overview

Cotizador Online OSPADEP provides access to plans from multiple leading healthcare providers (prestadores) in Argentina. Each provider offers different plan tiers, networks, and pricing structures. This guide helps you understand the differences and choose the right plan for your needs.

## How to Compare Plans

<Steps>
  <Step title="Consider Your Budget">
    Start with your monthly budget range. Plans range from ~$70,000 to over $900,000 per month depending on provider, plan tier, and family composition.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Evaluate Provider Networks">
    Different providers have different hospital and doctor networks. Check which providers are available in your area:
    - OMINT: Tiered networks (2500, 4500, 6500, 8500)
    - Swiss Medical: Comprehensive national network
    - Activa Salud: Focused network
    - MEDIFE: Wide network across Argentina
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Coverage Needs">
    Consider your family's specific healthcare needs:
    - **Basic coverage**: Consultations, emergencies, basic studies
    - **Comprehensive coverage**: Add dental, preventive medicine
    - **Premium coverage**: Add private rooms, high-complexity studies, international coverage
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Factor in Family Size">
    - **Solo/Pareja**: All providers competitive
    - **With children**: OMINT offers better child discounts than Swiss Medical
    - **Large families**: Consider per-capita pricing carefully
  </Step>
</Steps>
